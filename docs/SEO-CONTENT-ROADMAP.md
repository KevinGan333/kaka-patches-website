## Page Template

Each page should include:

- **H1** matching the target keyword
- **SEO title** with keyword + " | KaKa Patches"
- **Meta description** 140–160 chars with keyword + CTA
- **H2 sections** covering the topic in depth
- **Internal links** to related product pages and `/request-a-quote`
- **FAQ** section with 4–6 questions
- **CTA** — Request a Quote

## Internal Linking Strategy

- Product pages link to related resource guides
- Blog posts link to product pages and `/request-a-quote`
- Resource guides link to product pages and blog posts
- All pages link to `/request-a-quote`
